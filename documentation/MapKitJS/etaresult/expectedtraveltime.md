# expectedTravelTime

**Framework**: MapKit JS  
**Kind**: property

The estimated travel time in seconds, including delays due to traffic.

**Availability**:
- MapKit JS 5.46+

## Declaration

```swift
expectedTravelTime: number;
```

#### Discussion

The [`expectedTravelTime`](etaresult/expectedtraveltime.md) reflects the estimated time, in seconds, it takes to complete the route including traffic delay estimates.
