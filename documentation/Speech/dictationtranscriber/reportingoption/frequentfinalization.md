# DictationTranscriber.ReportingOption.frequentFinalization

**Framework**: Speech  
**Kind**: case

Biases the transcriber towards responsiveness, resulting in more frequent but also less accurate finalized results.

**Availability**:
- iOS 26.0+
- iPadOS 26.0+
- Mac Catalyst 26.0+
