# stop()

**Framework**: Multipeer Connectivity  
**Kind**: method

Stops advertising the service provided by a local peer and stops the assistant.

**Availability**:
- iOS 7.0+
- iPadOS 7.0+
- Mac Catalyst 13.1+
- macOS 10.10+
