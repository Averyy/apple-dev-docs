# Text.TruncationMode.middle

**Framework**: SwiftUI  
**Kind**: case

Truncate in the middle of the line.

**Availability**:
- iOS 13.0+
- iPadOS 13.0+
- Mac Catalyst 13.0+
- macOS 10.15+
- tvOS 13.0+
- visionOS 1.0+
- watchOS 6.0+

## Declaration

```swift
case middle
```

#### Discussion

Use this kind of truncation to omit characters from the middle of the string. For example, you could truncate the English alphabet as “ab…yz”.
