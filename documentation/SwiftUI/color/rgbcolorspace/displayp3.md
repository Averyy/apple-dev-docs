## Declaration

```swift
case displayP3
```

#### Discussion

This color space uses the Digital Cinema Initiatives - Protocol 3 (DCI-P3) primary colors, a D65 white point, and the [`Color.RGBColorSpace.sRGB`](color/rgbcolorspace/srgb.md) transfer function.
