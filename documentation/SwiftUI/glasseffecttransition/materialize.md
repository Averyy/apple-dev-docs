# materialize

**Framework**: SwiftUI  
**Kind**: property

The materialize glass effect transition which will fade in content and animate in or out the glass material but will not attempt to match the geometry of any other glass effects.

**Availability**:
- iOS 26.0+
- iPadOS 26.0+
- Mac Catalyst 26.0+
- macOS 26.0+
- tvOS 26.0+
- watchOS 26.0+
