# AccessibilityZoomGestureAction.Direction

**Framework**: SwiftUI  
**Kind**: enum

A direction that matches the movement of a zoom gesture performed by an assistive technology, such as a swipe up and down in Voiceover’s zoom rotor.

**Availability**:
- iOS 16.0+
- iPadOS 16.0+
- Mac Catalyst 16.0+
