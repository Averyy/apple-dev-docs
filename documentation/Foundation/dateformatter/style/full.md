# DateFormatter.Style.full

**Kind**: case

**Availability**:

- iPadOS 2.0+
- Mac Catalyst 13.0+

- tvOS 9.0+

- watchOS 2.0+

## Declaration

```swift
case full
```

#### Discussion

Specifies a full style with complete details, such as “Tuesday, April 12, 1952 AD” or “3:30:42 PM Pacific Standard Time”.

Equal to `kCFDateFormatterFullStyle`.
