# Get a Creative

**Framework**: Apple Search Ads  
**Kind**: httpRequest

Fetches a creative by identifier.

**Availability**:
- Search Ads 5.0+

#### Discussion

Use this endpoint to fetch details of a [`Creative`](creative.md) using your `creativeId` in the resource path.

##### Payload Example Get a Creative
