# WKInterfaceObjectHorizontalAlignment.center

**Framework**: WatchKit  
**Kind**: case

Center alignment. The object’s content is centered horizontally in the container and extends equally to the left and right.

**Availability**:
- watchOS 2.0+

## Declaration

```swift
case center
```
