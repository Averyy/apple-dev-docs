# SKDownloadTimeRemainingUnknown

**Framework**: StoreKit  
**Kind**: var

Indicates that the system cannot determine how much time is needed to finish downloading the content.

**Availability**:
- iOS 6.0+
- iPadOS 6.0+
- Mac Catalyst 13.1+
- macOS 10.14+
- tvOS 9.0+
- watchOS 6.2+
