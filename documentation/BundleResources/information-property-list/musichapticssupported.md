# MusicHapticsSupported

**Framework**: Bundle Resources  
**Kind**: typealias

A Boolean value that indicates to the system that your app supports the Music Haptics feature.

**Availability**:
- iOS 18.0+
- iPadOS 18.0+

#### Discussion

To notify the system that your app supports the Music Haptics feature, add [`MusicHapticsSupported`](information-property-list/musichapticssupported.md) with a value of `YES` to your appâ€™s `Info.plist`. When you add this key to your `Info.plist`, your app appears in the list of apps that support Music Haptics in Settings. For more information, read [`Music Haptics`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/MediaAccessibility/music-haptics).
