# Integrating Your App with Siri Event Suggestions

**Framework**: SiriKit

Donate reservations and provide quick access to event details throughout the system.

**Availability**:
- iOS 14.0+
- iPadOS 14.0+
- Mac Catalyst 14.0+
- Xcode 17.0+
