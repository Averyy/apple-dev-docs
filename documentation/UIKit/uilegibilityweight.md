# UILegibilityWeight

**Framework**: UIKit  
**Kind**: enum

Constants that indicate the weight to apply to text in your interface.

**Availability**:
- iOS 13.0+
- iPadOS 13.0+
- Mac Catalyst 13.1+
- tvOS 13.0+
- visionOS 1.0+
