# UICollectionLayoutSectionOrthogonalScrollingProperties.Bounce.always

**Framework**: UIKit  
**Kind**: case

The orthogonal scroll view bounces even if the content is smaller than its bounds.

**Availability**:
- iOS 17.0+
- iPadOS 17.0+
- Mac Catalyst 17.0+
- tvOS 17.0+
- visionOS 1.0+
