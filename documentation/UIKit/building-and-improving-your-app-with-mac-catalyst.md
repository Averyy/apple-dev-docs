# Building and improving your app with Mac Catalyst

**Framework**: UIKit

Improve your iPadOS app with Mac Catalyst by supporting native controls, multiple windows, sharing, printing, menus and keyboard shortcuts.

**Availability**:
- iOS 15.0+
- iPadOS 15.0+
- Mac Catalyst 15.0+
- Xcode 13.0+

#### Overview

> **Note**: This sample code project is associated with WWDC21 sessions: [`10052: What’s New in Mac Catalyst`](https://developer.apple.comhttps://developer.apple.com/wwdc21/10052/), [`10053: Qualities of a Great Mac Catalyst App`](https://developer.apple.comhttps://developer.apple.com/wwdc21/10053/) and [`10056: Qualities of Great iPad and iPhone Apps on Macs with M1`](https://developer.apple.comhttps://developer.apple.com/wwdc21/10056/).
