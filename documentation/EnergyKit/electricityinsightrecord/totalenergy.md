# totalEnergy

**Framework**: EnergyKit  
**Kind**: property

The total energy either consumed or generated in the date range.

**Availability**:
- iOS 26.0+ (Beta)
- iPadOS 26.0+ (Beta)

## Declaration


Represents the total electrical energy consumed or generated by the device in the date range for devices that report it.
