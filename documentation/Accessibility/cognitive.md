# Cognitive

**Framework**: Accessibility

A person may have difficulty remembering a sequence of steps, or they may find an overly complex user interface difficult to process and manage.

#### Overview

People who have cognitive impairments or challenges with maintaining focus can use Appleâ€™s cognitive accessibility features to stay focused, streamline tasks, or limit the number of decisions to make.
