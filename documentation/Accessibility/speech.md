# Speech

**Framework**: Accessibility

A person may have a speech disability or prefer to connect without using their voice.

#### Overview

People who are unable to speak, have a speech disability, or prefer to communicate through text can use Appleâ€™s speech accessibility features to communicate through their Apple devices.
