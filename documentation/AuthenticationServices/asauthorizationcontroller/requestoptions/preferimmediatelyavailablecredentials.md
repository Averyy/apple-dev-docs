# preferImmediatelyAvailableCredentials

**Framework**: Authentication Services  
**Kind**: property

Tells the authorization controller to prefer credentials that are immediately available on the local device.

**Availability**:
- iOS 16.0+
- iPadOS 16.0+
- Mac Catalyst 16.0+
- macOS 13.0+
- visionOS 1.0+
