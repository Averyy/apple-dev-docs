# HKUpdateFrequency.hourly

**Framework**: HealthKit  
**Kind**: case

The system launches your app at most once an hour in response to changes.

**Availability**:
- iOS 8.0+
- iPadOS 8.0+
- Mac Catalyst 13.0+
- macOS 13.0+
- visionOS 1.0+
- watchOS 2.0+
