# GLKLightingType.perPixel

**Framework**: GLKit  
**Kind**: case

Indicates that the inputs to the lighting calculation are interpolated across a triangle and the lighting calculations are performed at each fragment.

**Availability**:
- iOS 5.0+
- iPadOS 5.0+
- macOS 10.8+
- tvOS 9.0+
