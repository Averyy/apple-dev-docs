# ImageCropAndScaleAction.centerCrop

**Framework**: Vision  
**Kind**: case

An action that scales the image and maintains the aspect ratio to fit on the short side and crop centered on the long side.

**Availability**:
- iOS 18.0+

- Mac Catalyst 18.0+
