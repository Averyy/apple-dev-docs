# AVCaptureColorSpace.HLG_BT2020

**Framework**: AVFoundation  
**Kind**: case

The BT.2020 wide color space that uses Illuminant D65 as the white point and Hybrid Log-Gamma (HLG) as the transfer function.

**Availability**:
- iOS 14.1+
- iPadOS 14.1+
- Mac Catalyst 14.1+
- tvOS 17.0+
