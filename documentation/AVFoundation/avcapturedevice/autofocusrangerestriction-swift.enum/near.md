# AVCaptureDevice.AutoFocusRangeRestriction.near

**Framework**: AVFoundation  
**Kind**: case

The device primarily attempts to focus on subjects near the camera.

**Availability**:
- iOS 7.0+
- iPadOS 7.0+
- Mac Catalyst 14.0+
- tvOS 17.0+

#### Discussion
