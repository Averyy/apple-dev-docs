# AVCaptureCameraLensSmudgeDetectionStatus.smudgeNotDetected

**Framework**: AVFoundation  
**Kind**: case

Indicates that the most recent detection found no smudge on the camera lens.

**Availability**:
- iOS 26.0+
- iPadOS 26.0+
- Mac Catalyst 26.0+
- macOS 26.0+
- tvOS 26.0+
