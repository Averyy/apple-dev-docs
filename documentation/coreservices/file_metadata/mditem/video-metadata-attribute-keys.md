# Video Metadata Attribute Keys

**Framework**: Core Services

Metadata attribute keys that are common to video files.

## Topics

### Constants
- [let kMDItemAudioBitRate: CFString!](kmditemaudiobitrate.md)
  The audio bit rate. A CFNumber.
- [let kMDItemCodecs: CFString!](kmditemcodecs.md)
  The codecs used to encode/decode the media. A CFArray of CFStrings.
- [let kMDItemDeliveryType: CFString!](kmditemdeliverytype.md)
  The delivery type. Values are “Fast start” or “RTSP”. A CFString.
- [let kMDItemMediaTypes: CFString!](kmditemmediatypes.md)
  The media types present in the content. A CFArray of CFStrings.
- [let kMDItemStreamable: CFString!](kmditemstreamable.md)
  Whether the content is prepared for streaming. A CFBoolean.
- [let kMDItemTotalBitRate: CFString!](kmditemtotalbitrate.md)
  The total bit rate, audio and video combined, of the media. A CFNumber.
- [let kMDItemVideoBitRate: CFString!](kmditemvideobitrate.md)
  The video bit rate. A CFNumber.
- [let kMDItemDirector: CFString!](kmditemdirector.md)
  Directory of the movie. A CFString.
- [let kMDItemProducer: CFString!](kmditemproducer.md)
  Producer of the content. A CFString.
- [let kMDItemGenre: CFString!](kmditemgenre.md)
  Genre of the movie. A CFString.
- [let kMDItemPerformers: CFString!](kmditemperformers.md)
  Performers in the movie. A CFArray of CFStrings.
- [let kMDItemOriginalFormat: CFString!](kmditemoriginalformat.md)
  Original format of the movie. A CFString.
- [let kMDItemOriginalSource: CFString!](kmditemoriginalsource.md)
  Original source of the movie. A CFString.
