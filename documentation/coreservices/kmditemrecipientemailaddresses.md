# kMDItemRecipientEmailAddresses

**Framework**: Core Services  
**Kind**: data

This attribute indicates the recipients email addresses. (This is always the email address, and not the human readable version). A CFArray of CFStrings.

**Availability**:
- macOS 10.5+
