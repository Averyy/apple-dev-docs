# Get an Ad

**Framework**: Apple Ads  
**Kind**: httpRequest

Fetches an ad assigned to an ad group by identifier.

**Availability**:
- Search Ads 5.0+

#### Discussion

Use this endpoint to fetch an [`Ad`](ad.md) assigned to an ad group. Use your `adId` in the resource path.

##### Payload Example Get an Ad
