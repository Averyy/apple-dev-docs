# reverseEvents()

**Framework**: AVFAudio  
**Kind**: method

Reverses the order of all events in all music tracks, including the tempo track.

**Availability**:
- iOS 16.0+
- iPadOS 16.0+
- Mac Catalyst 16.0+
- macOS 13.0+
- tvOS 16.0+
- visionOS 1.0+
