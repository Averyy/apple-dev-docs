# CLKRelativeDateStyle.natural

**Framework**: ClockKit  
**Kind**: case

A natural date style.

**Availability**:
- watchOS 2.0+

## Declaration

```swift
case natural
```

#### Discussion

The text provider breaks the time interval into various components. For example, it formats 10 hours and 9 minutes as “10hrs 9min”.
