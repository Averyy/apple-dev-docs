# AppManagedUpdateBehaviorObject

**Framework**: Device Management  
**Kind**: dictionary

Specifies the update behavior of the apps installed from the App Store. Apps in packages are not automatically updated.

**Availability**:
- iOS 26.0+ (Beta)
- iPadOS 26.0+ (Beta)
- macOS 26.0+ (Beta)
- visionOS 26.0+ (Beta)
