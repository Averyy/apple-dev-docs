# kQLPreviewPDFPagesWithThumbnailsOnRightStyle

**Framework**: Quick Look  
**Kind**: var

The content of the PDF appears with thumbnails of all pages on the right side of the current page’s content.

**Availability**:
- macOS 10.5+
