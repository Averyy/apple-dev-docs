```swift
case degrees
```

#### Discussion

Typical range is from –180° through +180° and is intended as a general representation of geometric position, such as for audio sources in a three-dimensional coordinate system. See also the [`AudioUnitParameterUnit.phase`](audiounitparameterunit/phase.md) property.
