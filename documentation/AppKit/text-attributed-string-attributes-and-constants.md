### Constants

- [static let fontFamily: NSAccessibility.FontAttributeKey](nsaccessibility-swift.struct/fontattributekey/fontfamily.md)

- [static let visibleName: NSAccessibility.FontAttributeKey](nsaccessibility-swift.struct/fontattributekey/visiblename.md)
