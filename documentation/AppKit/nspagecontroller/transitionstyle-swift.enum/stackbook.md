# NSPageController.TransitionStyle.stackBook

**Framework**: AppKit  
**Kind**: case

Pages are stacked on top of each other. Pages animate out to the left to reveal the next page. Previous pages animate in from the left.

**Availability**:
- macOS 10.8+
