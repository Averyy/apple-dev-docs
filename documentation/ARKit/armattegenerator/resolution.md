# ARMatteGenerator.Resolution

**Framework**: ARKit  
**Kind**: enum

A resolution for a matte texture.

**Availability**:
- iOS 13.0+
- iPadOS 13.0+
- Mac Catalyst 13.1+

## Declaration

```swift
enum Resolution
```

#### Overview

You generate a matte texture every frame, specifying whether its resolution is the full size or half of the camera image.
