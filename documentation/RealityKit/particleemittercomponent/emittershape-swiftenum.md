## Topics

### Enumeration Cases
- [ParticleEmitterComponent.EmitterShape.box](particleemittercomponent/emittershape-swift.enum/box.md)
  Particles emit from an axis-aligned box that is centered on the origin.
- [ParticleEmitterComponent.EmitterShape.cone](particleemittercomponent/emittershape-swift.enum/cone.md)
  Particles emit from a cone with its apex pointing up along the y-axis, centered on the origin.
- [ParticleEmitterComponent.EmitterShape.cylinder](particleemittercomponent/emittershape-swift.enum/cylinder.md)
  Particles emit from a cylinder oriented vertically along the y-axis, centered on the origin.
- [ParticleEmitterComponent.EmitterShape.plane](particleemittercomponent/emittershape-swift.enum/plane.md)
  Particles emit from a 2D plane that aligns with the x and z axes.
- [ParticleEmitterComponent.EmitterShape.point](particleemittercomponent/emittershape-swift.enum/point.md)
  Particles emit from a single point on the origin.
- [ParticleEmitterComponent.EmitterShape.sphere](particleemittercomponent/emittershape-swift.enum/sphere.md)
  Particles emit from a sphere centered on the origin.
- [ParticleEmitterComponent.EmitterShape.torus](particleemittercomponent/emittershape-swift.enum/torus.md)
  Particles emit from a torus oriented along the z-axis, centered on the origin.
