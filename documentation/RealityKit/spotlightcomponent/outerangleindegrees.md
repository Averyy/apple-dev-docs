**Framework**: RealityKit  
**Kind**: property

**Availability**:

- iPadOS 13.0+
- Mac Catalyst 13.0+
- macOS 10.15+
- tvOS 26.0+ (Beta)
- visionOS 2.0+

## Declaration

