# preferredViewingMode

**Framework**: RealityKit  
**Kind**: property

Do we want to play stereo video in mono or stereo? Default is to play in stereo.

**Availability**:
- iOS 18.0+
- iPadOS 18.0+
- Mac Catalyst 18.0+
- macOS 15.0+
- visionOS 1.0+
