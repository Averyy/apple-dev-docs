# normal

**Framework**: RealityKit  
**Kind**: property

The normal of the contacting surfaces at the contact point. The normal direction points from the second shape to the first shape in scene coordinate space.

**Availability**:
- iOS 18.0+
- iPadOS 18.0+
- Mac Catalyst 18.0+
- macOS 15.0+
- visionOS 1.0+
