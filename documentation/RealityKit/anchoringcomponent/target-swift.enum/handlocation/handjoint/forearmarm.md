# forearmArm

**Framework**: RealityKit  
**Kind**: property

An anchor location at the elbow end of forearm on the back of hand.

**Availability**:
- visionOS 2.0+

## Declaration

```swift
static let forearmArm: AnchoringComponent.Target.HandLocation.HandJoint
```

#### Discussion

Content anchored this way has its positive y-axis in the direction of the dorsum of hand and its negative z-axis towards the side where the thumb is.
