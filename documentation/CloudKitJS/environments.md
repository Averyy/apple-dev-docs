# Environments

**Framework**: CloudKit JS

Constants to use when configuring the environment of containers.

## Topics

### Constants
- [DEVELOPMENT_ENVIRONMENT](cloudkit/development_environment.md)
  The container environment is not accessible by apps available on the store.
- [PRODUCTION_ENVIRONMENT](cloudkit/production_environment.md)
  The container environment is accessible by both development apps and apps available on the store.
