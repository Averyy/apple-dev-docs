# WidgetKit updates

**Framework**: Updates

Learn about important changes in WidgetKit.

#### Overview

Browse notable changes in [`WidgetKit`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/WidgetKit).

#### June 2025

##### General

- Support Liquid Glass by using the [`WidgetAccentedRenderingMode`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/WidgetKit/WidgetAccentedRenderingMode).
- Use WidgetKit push notifications to request timeline reloads from the system by registering a [`WidgetPushHandler`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/WidgetKit/WidgetPushHandler).
- Update your [`WidgetFamily.systemSmall`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/WidgetKit/WidgetFamily/systemSmall) widget to make sure it appears correctly in CarPlay.
- Create controls that use [`ControlWidgetButton`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/WidgetKit/ControlWidgetButton) to execute an action and [`ControlWidgetToggle`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/WidgetKit/ControlWidgetToggle) to toggle some state in your app in watchOS and macOS.

##### Vision Pro

- Widgets are available on Apple Vision Pro. Support [`elevated`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/WidgetKit/WidgetMountingStyle/elevated) and [`recessed`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/WidgetKit/WidgetMountingStyle/recessed) mounting styles, choose [`glass`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/WidgetKit/WidgetTexture/glass) or [`paper`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/WidgetKit/WidgetTexture/paper) textures, and specify layouts for each [`LevelOfDetail`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/WidgetKit/LevelOfDetail) to show more ore less information based on a personâ€™s proximity.

##### Apple Watch

- Offer a relevance-based watchOS widget that uses a [`RelevanceConfiguration`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/WidgetKit/RelevanceConfiguration).
- Let people configure your watchOS widget by offering a widget that uses [`AppIntentConfiguration`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/WidgetKit/AppIntentConfiguration).

#### June 2024

##### General

- Controls allow people to customize and configure Control Center, their Lock Screen, and the Action button with actions from your apps. Take advantage of [`ControlWidgetButton`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/WidgetKit/ControlWidgetButton) to execute an action and [`ControlWidgetToggle`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/WidgetKit/ControlWidgetToggle) to toggle some state in your app.
- Accent views of widgets on iOS and iPadOS using [`widgetAccentable(_:)`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/SwiftUI/View/widgetAccentable(_:)).

##### Apple Watch

- Create interactive widgets on watchOS using a [`Button`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/SwiftUI/Button) or [`Toggle`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/SwiftUI/Toggle) to perform actions without launching your app.
- Use an [`AccessoryWidgetGroup`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/WidgetKit/AccessoryWidgetGroup) container to provide up to three content views, optionally interactive, in a watchOS widget. Style [`AccessoryWidgetGroup`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/WidgetKit/AccessoryWidgetGroup) content views using [`circular`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/WidgetKit/AccessoryWidgetGroupStyle/circular) or [`roundedSquare`](https://developer.apple.com/documentation/WidgetKit/AccessoryWidgetGroupStyle/roundedSquare).
