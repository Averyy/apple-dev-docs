# eventScore

**Framework**: CarPlay  
**Kind**: property

The numeric score string for this team in the current event. Depending on the size of the car screen, a maximum of 3 to 5 characters may be displayed.

**Availability**:
- iOS 18.4+
- iPadOS 18.4+
- Mac Catalyst 18.4+
