# init(teamInitials:)

**Framework**: CarPlay  
**Kind**: init

If no team logo image is available, initialize a team logo with an abbreviation or initialism for this team.

**Availability**:
- iOS 18.4+
- iPadOS 18.4+
- Mac Catalyst 18.4+

## Declaration

```swift
init(teamInitials: String)
```

#### Discussion

A maximum of 3 characters may be displayed for the team initials.
