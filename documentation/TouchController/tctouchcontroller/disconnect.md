# disconnect()

**Framework**: Touch Controller  
**Kind**: method

Disconnects the touch controller from the app, preventing its controls from being drawn.

**Availability**:
- iOS 26.0+ (Beta)
- iPadOS 26.0+ (Beta)
- Mac Catalyst 26.0+ (Beta)
- visionOS 26.0+ (Beta)
